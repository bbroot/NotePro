## FAQ

### macOS says "app is damaged"
This only affects unsigned DMG versions. If you downloaded from the **Mac App Store**, this won't happen. For DMG downloads, go to System Settings → Privacy & Security → click "Open Anyway".

### Where are my notes stored?
All notes are stored locally on your device. No data is ever sent to external servers.

### Does Note Pro work offline?
Yes. Note Pro works entirely offline. No internet connection required.
